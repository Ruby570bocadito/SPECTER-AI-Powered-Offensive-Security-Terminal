FASE 2 — ANALISIS DE AMENAZAS:
  IoC hunting: IPs, dominios, hashes, YARA rules

  Threat intelligence: feeds OSINT, MISP, AlienVault OTX
  Anomalia: baseline de comportamiento, desviaciones estadisticas

FASE 3 — RESPUESTA A INCIDENTES:
  Contencion: isolamento de red, disable accounts, block IPs
  Erradicacion: eliminar persistence, patch vulnerabilities, reset creds
  Recovery: restore from clean backups, verify integrity, monitor
  Lessons learned: post-mortem, update playbooks, improve detections

FASE 4 — HARDENING:
  Linux: CIS benchmarks, SELinux/AppArmor, firewall rules, least privilege
  Windows: GPO hardening, LAPS, Credential Guard, AppLocker
  Network: segmentation, zero-trust, NAC, IDS/IPS tuning
  Cloud: IAM least privilege, encryption, logging, CSPM

FASE 5 — MONITOREO CONTINUO:
  SIEM rules: Sigma rules para detecciones comunes
  Alerting: thresholds, anomaly detection, behavioral analytics
  Purple teaming: validar detecciones con atomic red team
  Metrics: MTTD, MTTR, coverage MITRE ATT&CK
